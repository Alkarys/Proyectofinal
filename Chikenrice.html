Chiken With Rice

Chicken rice is a classic dish that, with its combination of comforting flavors and comforting texture, becomes a dish that is perfect for any occasion, from family meals to dinners with friends. Its simple preparation and delicious flavor make it a popular choice in many kitchens around the world.

Preparation Time

  • Approximately: 90-120 minutes.
  • Preparation: 25-35 minutes.
  • Cooking: 65-85 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Cook the vegetables: In a large pot or deep skillet, heat a little oil over medium heat. SautÃ© onion, garlic, bell pepper, carrots and peas until tender.
  2. Cook the rice: Add the rice to the pot and stir it with the vegetables to infuse it with the flavors. Pour the hot chicken broth over the rice and vegetables.
  3. Simmer: Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover the pot with a lid, and let the rice simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until it is tender and the liquid has been absorbed.
  4. Add the chicken: Once the rice is cooked, place the chicken pieces into the pot and gently mix them with the rice.
  5. Simmer: Cover the pot again with the lid and let the chicken heat through in the rice for a few more minutes over low heat.
  6. Serve: Remove from the heat and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve hot and enjoy this delicious chicken with rice.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Long-grain white rice
  • Chicken broth (you can use homemade or store-bought)
  • Onion, Carrots, diced
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Bell pepper (any color), diced
  • Peas (fresh or frozen)
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
