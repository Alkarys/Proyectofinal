Mondongo

Mondongo, a traditional dish popular in various Latin American countries, particularly in the Caribbean and South America, is a hearty and flavorful stew made primarily from beef tripe (the lining of the cow's stomach) cooked with a variety of vegetables and spices.At its core, mondongo is a dish that celebrates the art of slow cooking and the rich flavors of its ingredients.

Preparation Time

  • Approximately 150-180 minutes.
  • Preparation: 30-45 minutes.
  • Cooking: 120-190 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the tripe: Clean the beef tripe thoroughly under cold running water to remove any impurities. Cut it into small pieces and set aside.
  2. Boil the tripe: In a large pot, add the tripe pieces and cover with water or beef broth. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 1 hour, or until the tripe is tender. Skim off any foam or impurities that rise to the surface.
  3. Prepare the vegetables: While the tripe is cooking, chop the onion, garlic, bell pepper, tomatoes, carrot, and celery.
  4. Cook the vegetables: In a separate pan, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, bell pepper, tomatoes, carrot, and celery. SautÃ© until the vegetables are softened, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Combine the tripe and vegetables: Once the tripe is tender, add the sautÃ©ed vegetables to the pot with the tripe. Stir in the cumin, dried oregano, bay leaves, salt, and pepper. Add more water or beef broth if needed to cover the ingredients.
  6. Simmer and serve: Let the mondongo simmer over low heat for another 1-2 hours, stirring occasionally, until the flavors meld together and the tripe is very tender. Once cooked, remove the bay leaves, taste, and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Serve hot, garnished with chopped cilantro or parsley if desired, and with lime or lemon wedges on the side for squeezing over the stew.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds (about 900g) beef tripe, cleaned and cut into small pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water or beef broth
  • Chopped cilantro or parsley
  • Lime or lemon wedges for serving
